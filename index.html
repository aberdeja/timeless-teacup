Our Story

Timeless Teacup was originally started by two good friends in their rundown little cottage. Their goal was to bring a touch of magic to people's lives through their shared love of tea.

Customer Favorites

  • Dead Man's Drop - A bold flavoured tea, containing yarrow and hyssop to help you perk up when you're feeling under the weather.
  • Lazy Lavender - A sweetly flavoured tea, made from our freshest lavender; the perfect solution for those looking for a sound sleep.
